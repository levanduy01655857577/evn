Luxury Beachfront Hotel

Discover Navada Beach Hotel with unique experience right at the famous sight of Hon Chong with blue sea and white sand. The hotel features 153 elegant and luxurious rooms, together with modern equipment and harmonious combination of elegant Eurasian architecture. Bring comfort to the cozy atmosphere and a view that captures the entire ocean. The hotel is located opposite of Hon Chong Beach, just a few steps from the coast and about 100m from famous Hon Chong, where you enjoy the most beautiful sunrise in Nha Trang. With a staff of enthusiastic and professional service, we will give you the most comfortable and satisfied experience during your stay at the hotel. With a blend of indigenous charm and international standards, Navada Beach Hotel is the right choice for both business and leisure travelers and is recognized by many customers for its outstanding service quality and unique location. Come with us, you will enjoy the experience of a real holiday and unforgettable memories.
